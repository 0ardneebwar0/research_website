We are a research-focused community dedicated to advancing AI knowledge in Nepal and contributing to global scientific progress. While our primary mission is research and education, we occasionally provide consulting for research-oriented projects.

Our Mission

Building Nepal's AI research capacity and contributing to global scientific advancement through collaborative research, education, and knowledge sharing.

Research Excellence

We conduct cutting-edge AI research and publish high-quality academic papers that contribute to global knowledge and scientific progress.

Education & Mentorship

We're committed to educating the next generation of AI researchers and providing mentorship to build research capacity across Nepal.

Community Impact

Building a strong research community that can tackle Nepal's unique challenges through innovative AI solutions and collaborative research.

Our Values

The principles that guide our research and community building efforts.

Open Knowledge

  • We believe in open research and knowledge sharing to accelerate scientific progress
  • Our research findings are made available to the global research community
  • We encourage collaboration and mentorship across institutions

Social Impact

  • Research that addresses real challenges faced by Nepal and similar developing nations
  • Building local research capacity to reduce brain drain
  • Ethical AI development that benefits society

Collaboration

  • Fostering partnerships with international research institutions
  • Interdisciplinary research approaches
  • Mentoring emerging researchers and students

Excellence

  • Rigorous research methodologies and peer review processes
  • Continuous learning and adaptation to emerging technologies
  • Maintaining high standards in all our research endeavors

Occasional Consulting

While our primary focus is research and education, we occasionally provide consulting services for research-oriented projects that align with our mission. This includes collaboration with academic institutions, research organizations, and projects with significant educational or social impact value.
