Application Requirements

General Requirements

  • Educational Background: Relevant degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, or related field
  • Programming Skills: Proficiency in Python, R, or similar languages used in AI/ML
  • Research Interest: Clear motivation and interest in AI research areas
  • Communication: Good English writing and communication skills
  • Commitment: Ability to commit dedicated time for research activities

Required Documents

  • CV/Resume: Updated CV highlighting relevant experience and skills
  • Statement of Purpose: 500-word essay explaining research interests and goals
  • Academic Transcripts: Official or unofficial transcripts from relevant institutions
  • Portfolio/Projects: Links to GitHub, projects, or previous research work
  • References: Contact information for 2-3 academic or professional references
