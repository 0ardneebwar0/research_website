Research Collaborations

We selectively engage in consulting projects that align with our research mission and contribute to academic advancement.

We Consider

  • Academic Institutions: Research collaboration with universities and research centers
  • Research Projects: Applied research with potential for publication and knowledge sharing
  • Social Impact: Projects addressing Nepal's specific challenges through AI innovation
  • Capacity Building: Initiatives that build local AI research capabilities

We Don't Offer

  • Commercial AI Solutions: Standard business AI implementations or product development
  • General Tech Consulting: Non-research focused technology consulting services
  • Quick Fixes: Short-term projects without research or educational value
  • Proprietary Work: Projects that restrict knowledge sharing or publication
